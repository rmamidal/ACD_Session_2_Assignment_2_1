Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a fraying pan over a medidum heat
  2. Genlty mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once the buttur has melted add the cream and eggs
  4. Using the spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to center every 20 seconds( as if you are making an omlet)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
